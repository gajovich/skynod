Company Mission

Skynode was established in May 2017 with the simple goal of making quality hosting services available and affordable for everyone. Over the past year, Skynode has built a reputation of being an ideal provider for people looking for game hosting for a variety of reasons. Whatever our clients are looking for, we're usually able to provide it!

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section, where we address common queries about Skynode's game server hosting services. If you have questions, you're likely to find answers here. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated support team for personalized assistance.

  • What is Minecraft Server Hosting?

    Game server hosting is a service that allows you to run your own gaming server, enabling you to create and customize your gaming world. You need it to provide a stable and fast platform for your players to enjoy your game.

  • How is Skynode so cheap?

    Skynode is able to offer cost-effective server hosting due to our efficient infrastructure. We prioritize providing high-quality services without unnecessary overhead, allowing us to offer affordable pricing to our customers while maintaining exceptional performance. Our goal is to make top-tier game server hosting accessible to a wide range of gamers.

  • Can I change my server's version or modpack?

    Absolutely! You have the flexibility to switch your server's version or modpack at any time through our user-friendly control panels.

  • Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

    At Skynode, we offer a risk-free 14-day refund policy. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first two weeks, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.

  • How are we different from other hosting providers?

    Skynode is known for its top-notch performance, global server locations, easy customization, and reliable support. Our dedication to your gaming experience sets us apart from the rest.

  • Can I upgrade or downgrade my server plan?

    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your server plan at any time to suit your needs. Contact our support team for assistance with this process.

  • Can I add plugins to the server

    Yes, you can easily add plugins to your Skynode game server to customize your gaming experience. Our user-friendly control panels make it simple to install and manage plugins to tailor your server to your preferences.

  • Are you affiliated with Mojang?

    Skynode is an independent game server hosting provider and is not affiliated with Mojang, the creators of Minecraft. We offer services to enhance your gaming experience but are not directly associated with the game's development or ownership.
